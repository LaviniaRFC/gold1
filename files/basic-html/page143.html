From: rewales@cnanet.com To: you@cnanet.com Subject: Hello!
Dear friend,
We hope this  nds you well and wish you a Merry Christmas and a happy, healthy New Year.
As evidenced by the absence of a letter in last year’s Christmas greeting, this parenting thing is pretty all-consuming. Here’s a little update on the boys.
Joshua is 5 years old now. He started kindergarten across the street this year. He loves his teacher, Mrs. Boudreau. His favorite classes are gym, art, and music. He’s made lots of new friends and he’s learning to read. I call him a reading maniac. He also likes learning about animals, the planets, learning how things work and doing experiments.
Conrad, who’s 2 years old now, loves to play with his big brother and repeats everything he says and does. He started preschool on Fridays and he’s learning his ABCs, counting, colors, and songs. He likes to play with Play-Doh, listen to stories, and jump on his trampoline.
After two years at his Wall Street analyst job, Luke decided to make a move which, in retrospect, was a very good thing given what’s happening on Wall Street now. He has always been interested in energy conservation and is now working for NYSERDA (New York State Energy Research and Development Authority), managing investments and projects to improve the ways we use electricity.
After six years as a professional developer for teachers, I resigned to stay at home with the boys for the remaining brief and precious preschool years. And somehow my life is as busy and full as ever. I have become involved in our school district in various ways, from volunteering in Joshua’s classroom to being on a budget advisory group for the district. And I’m still writing English test items and multicultural children’s book reviews.
Well, that’s all the news from us. Hope you are all doing well. We wish you all a very joyful Christmas!
Love, Rebecca
